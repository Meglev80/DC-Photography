Meet Danielle

What makes my work special.

I have been taking pictures most of my life, In 2017 I decided that I was going to try a more professional approach to photographing my famly, this is when it became my passion. My three boys who inspired me, reminded me that I would be capturing life's special moments. I absolutely love what I do today.

Dedicated. Intentional. Caring. Joyful. Artistic. These are the words used to describe what I strive towards continuously. The same sentiments are often shared by couples and famlies in thank you notes I receive after many months of communication back and forth, and after documenting their day. Caring deeply about each couple interacted with, taking time to get to know you, which enables me to capture your moments authentically, all the while preserving your day with passion and attention to detail.

Stories of our Lives

Weddings & Engagements

Wedding photographer is always a challenge due to every event being unique. From different lighting situations, size of venues, snow to rain. With all of these usually uncontrollable circumstances, I do my best to ensure your special day is captured perfectly! Metro Detroit is a special place to me and I invite you to contact me to discuss your upcoming wedding. We can chat about photography ideas, styles, where your wedding venue is located, and if we think my wedding photography would be a good fit for your expectations.

Family

Your family is the most important aspect of your life. Having people you love with all your heart to surround you at all times is something of great value. Whether you are just starting your new life with your loved one, or you have had years to get to know each other, capturing those precious moments creates a timeless piece of art that will forever remind you of the memories you have shared.

Seniors & Grads

My style is natural and relaxed and typically involves lots of laughter. I like to highlight what makes you... YOU.
Most of us aren't full-time models and appreciate some guidance when being photographed. I'll give you plenty of direction so that you get photos that are fun and candid. I am open to any ideas for your session!

Baby & Kids

Newborn & Children photography is unlike any other photography session. When clients hire me they are trusting me to care for their baby or kids during their session. Not only should your photographer be trained to safely pose your baby, they should also understand the intricacies of digital editing as many photos are combined to create those gorgeous images.
